Who we are

We specialise in manufacturing and installing fences, gates and wickets in Tallinn and Harjumaa since 2021. We work with wood, profiled sheet and welded 3D mesh, fit gate automation and intercoms, and repair existing structures. Our craftsmen have over 15 years of experience with fences and gates.

We take the whole process off your hands: we come for a free measurement, buy the materials, install and hand over the finished job. We name the price up front. No hidden extras or surprises.

A new fence around the cottage, a sliding gate with automation, or a full perimeter. And unlike many, we work year-round. Even in winter.
