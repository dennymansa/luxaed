How we work

Four simple steps

1

You send a request

One call or message. And we take the fence off your hands. We come for a free measurement at a time that suits you.

2

We advise & quote

We suggest the material and solution for your plot and budget and give an exact price. No surprises on the invoice.

3

We install with care

We set the posts, sections, gates and automation. We keep you posted at every stage.

4

We hand it over

When the work is done we review the result together with you and hand over the finished job. With care and maintenance tips.

Honest about prices

What's always included and what depends on the project

The price depends on the material, fence length, terrain and gate complexity. So there's no fixed price list. We give the exact price after a free measurement.

Always included

  • Site measurement
  • Advice on material and construction
  • A quote before the work begins
  • Installing posts and sections
  • Fitting the hardware and, if needed, automation
  • Checking the gates after installation

Depends on the project

  • Choice of material (wood, corrugated sheet, 3D mesh)
  • Fence length and height, number of gates
  • Terrain complexity and base preparation
  • Gate automation and intercom. Optional
  • Removing the old fence
