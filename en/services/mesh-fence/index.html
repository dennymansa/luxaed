What you get

Why a 3D fence

A welded panel with bends (3D) holds its shape without sagging or wind load and looks modern. A great fit for houses, townhouses and grounds.

  • Strong welded panels with stiffening ribs
  • Galvanised + powder-coated — no rust
  • Anthracite RAL 7016 and other colours
  • Good visibility, minimal wind load
  • Fast mounting on posts
  • 2D and 3D panels available

Honest about pricing

What affects the price

There is no fixed price list — we quote after a free on-site measurement. Here's what's always included and what affects the total.

Always included

  • On-site measurement
  • Installation of galvanised posts
  • Mounting of welded panels and fixings
  • Levelling to the terrain
  • Post-installation check
  • We work year-round, including winter
  • Warranty on completed work

Affects the price

  • Fence length and panel height (1.23–2.03 m)
  • Panel type (2D/3D) and colour
  • Terrain and groundwork
  • Number of gates and wickets
  • Removal of the old fence
