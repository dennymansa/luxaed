What you get

Gate automation at any scale

We design and install gate automation at any scale: from a single pedestrian gate to a full entry system for a large property. One remote or key fob controls every gate and barrier on the plot; open it from the remote, a phone call, an app or a code. We add and remove numbers at any time and connect intercoms and video call panels. We fit drives with rolling-code encryption, protected against interception by common code-grabber scanners. We also service and upgrade existing systems.

  • Projects of any scale: from a pedestrian gate to a whole property
  • One remote for every gate and barrier on the plot
  • Control: remote, phone call, app or code
  • Rolling-code encryption: protected against remote grabbers
  • Add and remove numbers at any time
  • Intercoms and video call panels
  • Service and upgrade of existing systems

Honest about pricing

What affects the price

There is no fixed price list. We quote after a free on-site measurement. Here's what's always included and what affects the total.

Always included

  • Measurement of the entrance
  • Making the gate and pedestrian gate
  • Installation and levelling
  • Automation mounting and setup
  • Intercom connection, function check
  • We work year-round, including winter
  • Warranty on completed work

Affects the price

  • Gate type (sliding / swing)
  • Leaf width and weight
  • Automation drive brand
  • Intercom, barrier and extra options
  • Infill (wood, sheet, mesh panel)
