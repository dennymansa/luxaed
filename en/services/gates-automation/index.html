Honest about pricing

What affects the price

There is no fixed price list — we quote after a free on-site measurement. Here's what's always included and what affects the total.

Always included

  • Measurement of the entrance
  • Making the gate and wicket
  • Installation and levelling
  • Automation mounting and setup
  • Intercom connection, function check
  • We work year-round, including winter
  • Warranty on completed work

Affects the price

  • Gate type (sliding / swing)
  • Leaf width and weight
  • Automation drive brand
  • Intercom and extra options
  • Infill (wood, sheet, mesh panel)
